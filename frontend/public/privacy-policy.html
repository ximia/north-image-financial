This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you share with us when you visit our website or submit a quote request.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a form on our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number

  • State of residence
  • Coverage preferences and health-related details you voluntarily provide

We only collect information you choose to provide to us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Follow up with you about life insurance and final expense coverage options

We do not use your information for any purpose unrelated to serving your insurance needs.

3. SMS Messaging

By submitting your information through our website, you consent to receive text messages from Colby Lutz at North Image Financial regarding your insurance inquiry.

Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. For help, reply HELP or call (360) 991-3360.

SMS opt-in information and consent data are never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties for any purpose.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used exclusively by North Image Financial to respond to your inquiry and provide insurance guidance.

We may share your information only if required by law or regulatory authority, or with your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected — typically for the duration of your active inquiry or client relationship — or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your information at any time (see Section 7).

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for functionality purposes only, such as remembering form progress or maintaining site performance. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to submit a quote request.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS or email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly using the information below. We will respond to all requests promptly.
